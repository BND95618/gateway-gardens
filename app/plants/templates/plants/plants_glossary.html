Cultivar

A specific plant variety that has been intentionally bred by humans to possess desirable characteristics, meaning it is cultivated and maintained through human intervention; essentially, a cultivar is a "cultivated variety" of a plant species. The scientific naming is as follows: Genus species 'Cultivar'

Sunset Zones

Sunset climate zones consider the total climate: length of growing season, timing and amount of rainfall winter lows, summer highs, wind, and humidity.
